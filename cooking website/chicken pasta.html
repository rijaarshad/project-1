Chicken Pasta Recipe

This Chicken Pasta is a delicious blend of tender chicken, fresh vegetables, and flavorful spices. Perfect for a quick dinner, it’s both healthy and mouth-watering!

Ingredients

Chicken & Vegetable Pasta:

  • Oil 2 tbs
  • Lehsan (Garlic) crushed 1 tsp
  • Hari mirch (Green chili) chopped 1
  • Chicken boneless cubes ¾ Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) chopped 1 small
  • Gajar (Carrot) cubes ¼ Cup
  • Matar (Peas) boiled ¼ Cup
  • Kernel corns boiled ¼ Cup
  • Tamatar (Tomato) cubes ½ Cup
  • Lal mirch powder (Red chili powder) ½ tsp or to taste
  • Namak (Salt) ½ tsp or to taste
  • Haldee powder (Turmeric powder) ¼ tsp
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 1-2 tbs
  • Spiral pasta boiled 2 Cups
  • Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ¼ tsp

Ingredients

Chicken Cheese Pasta:

  • Oil 2 tbs
  • Lehsan (Garlic) crushed 1 tsp
  • Hari mirch (Green chili) chopped 1
  • Chicken boneless strips ½ Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) chopped 2 tbs
  • Tomato sauce ½ Cup
  • Lal mirch powder (Red chili powder) ½ tsp
  • Namak (Salt) ½ tsp
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 1-2 tbs
  • Spiral pasta boiled 2 Cups
  • Penny pasta boiled 2 Cups
  • Cheddar cheese grated ½ Cup
  • Matar (Peas) boiled ¼ Cup
  • Kernel corns boiled ¼ Cup
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 1 tbs


Directions

Chicken & Vegetable Pasta:

  1. In a pot, add oil, garlic, green chili, and mix well.
  2. Add chicken, onion, and mix well until it changes color.
  3. Add carrot and mix well.
  4. Add peas, kernel corns, tomato, red chili powder, salt, turmeric powder, and mix well.
  5. Add fresh coriander and mix.
  6. Add boiled spiral pasta and give it a good mix.
  7. Add black pepper crushed & serve.

Directions

Chicken Cheese Pasta:

  1. In a pot, add oil, garlic, green chili, and mix well.
  2. Add chicken and mix well until it changes color.
  3. Add onion and mix well.
  4. Add tomato sauce, red chili powder, and salt, mix well, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add boiled penny pasta and give it a good mix.
  6. Add cheddar cheese and mix well.
  7. Add fresh coriander and serve.
