Creamy Methi Dahi Phulki

Phulki is a delicious crispy snack served with creamy yogurt and tangy Gur Imli Ki Chutney. It's a perfect dish for parties or an evening snack.

Directions

Prepare Phulki:

  1. In a blender, add yellow lentil, garlic, red chili powder, pink salt, water, and blend well.
  2. Take out in a bowl, add baking soda & whisk well until fluffy, then transfer to a piping bag.
  3. In a wok, heat cooking oil, place a wire rack over hot oil, and pipe out phulki batter over it & fry on medium flame until golden & crispy.
  4. In a large bowl, add water & pink salt and mix well.
  5. Add fried phulki, cover & let them soak until soft (15 minutes) then take out from water and squeeze gently to remove excess water & set aside.
  6. Note: To check the consistency of batter, drop 1 tbsp of batter in water, if it sinks, beat again for 1-2 minutes. If batter floats, it's ready to use.

Prepare Creamy Yogurt:

  1. In a bowl, add yogurt, cream, and sugar, whisk well until sugar is dissolved, and set aside.

Prepare Gur Imli ki Chutney:

  1. In a saucepan, add jaggery, tamarind pulp, red chili powder, ginger powder, pink salt, red chili crushed & water. Mix well and bring it to a boil and cook on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, add cornflour, water, and mix well.
  3. Now add dissolved cornflour, mix well & cook until it thickens.
  4. Let it cool completely.
