Beef Burger Recipe

This delicious Beef Burger is packed with flavorful beef patties, creamy mushroom & onion sauce, and a tangy BBQ sauce. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

Beef Patty:

  • Beef qeema (Mince) 500g
  • Anday ki zardi (Egg yolk) 1
  • BBQ sauce 1 tbs
  • Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed ½ tsp
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
  • Namak (Salt) ½ tbs or to taste
  • Lehsan powder (Garlic powder) 1 tsp
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 2 tbs
  • Cooking oil 1-2 tbs
  • Cheddar cheese slices 2
  • Pepperoni slices as required
  • Makhan (Butter) 1 tsp
  • Burger buns

Mushroom & Onion Sauce:

  • Makhan (Butter) 1-2 tbs
  • Mushrooms sliced 1 Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) sliced 1 large
  • Namak (Salt) ¼ tsp or to taste
  • Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ¼ tsp
  • Cream 3 tbs

Special BBQ Burger Sauce:

  • Mayonnaise ¾ Cup
  • Tomato ketchup ¼ Cup
  • BBQ sauce 2 tbs
  • Mustard paste 1 tbs
  • Sirka (Vinegar) ½ tbs
  • Lehsan powder (Garlic powder) ¼ tsp
  • Pyaz powder (Onion powder) ¼ tsp
  • Namak (Salt) 1 pinch

Assembling:

  • Burger bun
  • Special BBQ Burger sauce
  • Iceberg
  • Pyaz (Onion) rings
  • Tamatar (Tomato) slices
  • Double Beef patty with cheese
  • Prepared Mushroom & Onion sauce
  • Prepared Special BBQ Burger sauce
  • Mustard paste


Directions

Prepare Beef Patty:

  1. In a bowl, mix beef mince, egg yolk, BBQ sauce, red chilli crushed, black pepper powder, salt, garlic powder, fresh coriander.
  2. Take a small quantity (approx. 90g) and make a ball.
  3. Place a butter paper on a clean surface, place the ball & another butter paper on top. Flatten with heavy weight to make a patty (makes 6 patties).
  4. Heat a cast iron grill pan, grease with oil, and place 2 patties. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then flip and place cheddar cheese slices on each patty.
  5. Grease the pan with oil, place pepperoni slices, and fry for a minute on both sides.
  6. Now, place a pepperoni slice on each patty and set aside.

Prepare Mushroom & Onion Sauce:

  1. In a frying pan, melt butter, add mushrooms, and sauté for a minute.
  2. Add onions, mix well, and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add salt, black pepper, and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add cream, mix well, and set aside.

Prepare Special BBQ Burger Sauce:

  1. In a bowl, add mayonnaise, ketchup, BBQ sauce, mustard paste, vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt. Whisk well.

Assemble Burger:

  1. Toast the burger buns with butter and set aside.
  2. On the bottom bun, add prepared BBQ sauce, iceberg, onion rings, tomato slices, double beef patties with cheese slices, prepared mushroom & onion sauce, mustard paste, and top with another bun.
  3. Serve with potato fries and tomato ketchup.
