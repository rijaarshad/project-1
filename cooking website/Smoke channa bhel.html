Smoke Channa Bhel Recipe

Smoke Channa Bhel is a delicious and smoky snack made with chickpeas, spices, and chutneys. A perfect dish for a light evening snack or a party starter.

Ingredients

Prepare Green Chutney:

  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) 1 Cup
  • Podina (Mint leaves) 1 Cup
  • Lehsan (Garlic) cloves 2
  • Zeera (Cumin seeds) 1 tsp
  • Himalayan pink salt ½ tsp or to taste
  • Hari mirch (Green chillies) 6-7
  • Imli pulp (Tamarind pulp) 3-4 tbs
  • Water ¼ Cup or as required

Prepare Imli ki Meethi Chutney:

  • Gur (Jaggery) 250g
  • Lal mirch powder (Red chili powder) ¼ tsp or to taste
  • Zeera (Cumin seeds) roasted & crushed ½ tsp
  • Kala namak (Black salt) ½ tsp
  • Adrak powder (Ginger powder) ½ tbs
  • Imli pulp (Tamarind pulp) 3 tbs
  • Water 1 Cup or as required
  • Cornflour 2 tsp
  • Water 1-2 tbs

Prepare Bhel Puri:

  • Murmuray (Puffed rice) 3 Cups
  • Mixed nimco 1 & ½ Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) chopped 1 medium
  • Tamatar (Tomato) deseeded & chopped 1 medium
  • Aloo (Potatoes) boiled & cubes 2 medium
  • Kheera (Cucumber) deseeded & chopped 1 medium
  • Kalay chanay (Black chickpeas) boiled 1 & ½ Cup
  • Chana daal (Split bengal gram) 3 tbs (soaked for 6 hours) optional
  • Crushed mixed pickle 1 tbs with oil
  • Hari mirch (Green chillies) chopped 2
  • Adrak (Ginger) grated 1 tsp
  • Kala namak (Black salt) 1 tsp
  • Chaat masala 1 tsp
  • Tatri (Citric acid) ¼ tsp
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped handful
  • Lemon juice 2-3 tbs
  • Koyla (Charcoal) for smoke

Directions

Prepare Green Chutney:

  1. In a blender jug, add fresh coriander, mint leaves, garlic, cumin seeds, pink salt, green chillies, tamarind pulp, and water. Blend well & set aside.

Prepare Imli ki Meethi Chutney:

  1. In a saucepan, add jaggery, red chili powder, cumin seeds, black salt, ginger powder, tamarind pulp & water.
  2. Turn on the flame, mix well, bring it to boil & cook on medium flame until jaggery is dissolved.
  3. In cornflour, add water & mix well.
  4. Now add dissolved cornflour, mix well & cook on low flame until it thickens.
  5. Let it cool.

Prepare Bhel Puri:

  1. In a wok, add puffed rice & dry roast on low flame until crispy & light golden (3-4 minutes).
  2. Take out in a bowl, add mixed nimco, onion, tomato, potatoes, cucumber, black chickpeas, split bengal gram, crushed mixed pickle, green chillies, ginger, black salt, chaat masala, citric acid, fresh coriander, lemon juice, 1-2 tbs of prepared imli ki meethi chutney, 1-2 tbs of prepared green chutney & mix well.
  3. Give coal smoke for 2 minutes & serve!
