Chicken Biryani

This flavorful and aromatic Chicken Biryani is made with aromatic spices, tender chicken, and perfectly cooked rice. It's a dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends!

Ingredients

Rice Ingredients:

  • Falak Extreme basmati rice 750g (4 Cups)
  • Water 2 litre
  • Darchini (Cinnamon sticks) 2-3
  • Badiyan ka phool (Star anise) 1
  • Tez paat (Bay leaves) 3
  • Namak (Salt) 1 & ½ tbs or to taste
  • Hari elaichi (Green cardamom) 5-6
  • Hari mirch (Green chillies) 4-5
  • Sirka (Vinegar) 1 & ½ tbs
  • Tamatar (Tomatoes) cubes 2 large
  • Lehsan (Garlic) 7-8
  • Adrak (Ginger) 2 inch piece
  • Hari mirch (Green chillies) 6-7
  • Jaifil & Javatri powder (Nutmeg & Mace powder) ½ tsp
  • Saunf (Fennel seeds) crushed 1 tsp
  • Laung powder (Clove powder) ¼ tsp
  • Cooking oil ½ Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) sliced 2 medium

Chicken Ingredients:

  • Sabut kali mirch (Black peppercorns) ½ tsp
  • Laung (Cloves) 6-7
  • Zeera (Cumin seeds) 1 tbs
  • Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) 1 tbs or to taste
  • Haldee powder (Turmeric powder) ½ tbs
  • Dhania powder (Coriander powder) 1 & ½ tbs
  • Garam masala powder 1 tsp
  • Namak (Salt) 2 tsp or to taste
  • Lemon juice 3 tbs
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) 1/4 Cup
  • Chicken 750g
  • Aloo bukhara (Dried plums) 7-8
  • Dahi (Yogurt) whisked ½ Cup
  • Pyaz (Onion) fried 2 tbs
  • Podina (Mint leaves) 1-2 tbs

Additional Ingredients:

  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) 1-2 tbs
  • Pyaz (Onion) fried 1-2 tbs
  • Podina (Mint leaves) 1-2 tbs
  • Water 2 tbs
  • Kewra water ½ tbs
  • Zarda ka rang (Yellow food color) 1/4 tsp
  • Ghee 1 & ½ tbs


Directions

Prepare Rice:

  1. In a bowl, add rice, water, wash thoroughly and soak for 30 minutes then strain soaked rice.
  2. In a pot, add water, cinnamon sticks, star anise, bay leaves, salt, green cardamom, green chillies, vinegar, mix well and bring it to boil.
  3. Add rice and boil until 3/4th done (8-10 minutes) then strain & set aside.

Prepare Chicken Gravy:

  1. In a chopper, add tomatoes, garlic, ginger, green chillies and chop well to make a paste & set aside.
  2. In a bowl, add nutmeg & mace powder, fennel seeds, clove powder, mix well & set aside.
  3. In a pot, add cooking oil, onions and fry until light golden.
  4. Add black peppercorns, cloves, cumin seeds and mix well.
  5. Now add tomato & chillies paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, salt, lemon juice, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add fresh coriander and mix well.
  7. Add chicken and mix well until it changes color.
  8. Add dried plums, yogurt, mix well and cook for 18-20 minutes & until oil separates.
  9. Take out half of the chicken gravy in a bowl for layering.

Assemble & Cook:

  1. In a pot, add fried onion, mint leaves, fresh coriander, half quantity of prepared spice mix, half quantity of 3/4th boiled rice, fried onion, mint leaves, fresh coriander, remaining spice mix, reserved chicken gravy, and remaining boiled rice.
  2. In water, add kewra water, yellow food color and mix well.
  3. In a pot, add dissolved food color, ghee, cover and steam cook on low flame for 12-15 minutes & serve!
